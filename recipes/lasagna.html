Homemade Lasagna Recipe

Description

This recipe has been perfected after much trial and error, much to my kitchen's dismay! I always loved lasagna and decided a few years back to put my own spin on it. Now I'm going share that recipe with all of you.

Making lasagna can be time consuming but trust me when I say the outcome is definitely worth it, after all there's a reason it's Garfield's favourite food!

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 tubes of tomato puree
  • Spices: parsley, basil, salt, Italian seasoning and black pepper
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Make the sauce
  2. Cook the lasagna sheets
  3. Cook the beef
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover tray and leave to bake
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving
